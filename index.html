About Me

I grew up in Canada and was raised by immigrant parents from the Philippines. As I was raised, a hard work ethic and honesty were major points that were instilled in me. I then attended university at the University of Manitoba, majoring in Human Resources. After attaining my degree and gathering some industry experience in Canada, I immigrated to the United States to be with my now Husband. We spent the next few years building our careers and expanding our portfolios in Las Vegas. Then, COVID hit and the world slowed down. This put things into perspective for us, so we started to build our family and decided that it was best to move back to my Husbands home in Minnesota to be closer to family. I maintain strong family values, and I believe that family is one of the most important things in our lives. However, family comes in various forms, not just outside of the workplace. I love to help companies develop family type environments within the workplace, as I believe this helps not only retain valuable team members, but helps attract new extraordinary individuals as well. Let me help you build those family like bonds within your organization and push the needle on what your teams can achieve!
